Captain CigArrr

End Assignment - Game Projects @ DAE

Trailer

Captain CigARRR is a game made by students for the "Game Projects" course in our second year at Digital Arts & Entertainment. We had 11 weeks to bring the idea to life, from brainstorming to the final polish.

The Game

You control a small ship in a cluster of islands, trying to survive waves of enemies. Collect resources and abilities to upgrade your ship and take on even more challenges. But be warned: the more you upgrade, the more infamous you become, and the more enemies will come after you! See how long you can survive this never-ending storm of chaos.

Controls

(You'll need a controller to play!) Use the left joystick to move your ship. Press Button B to interact with the seapost. Button Y fires your main cannons (at sea). Button X lets you shoot side cannons and the blunderbuss. Button A gives you a quick boost in the water or helps you roll on land. The shoulder buttons rotate the camera, while the Left Trigger activates your special ability. Press the Right Trigger to boost your ship using the sails.
