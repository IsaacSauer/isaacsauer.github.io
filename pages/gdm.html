Grand Dad Mania

Grand Dad Mania - Programming 2 @ DAE

Trailer

I created Grand Dad Mania as my final project for the "Programming 2" course. The assignment was to build a platformer game that met certain criteria in our grading rubric. Later on, I decided to add the Discord SDK to try out the Discord RPC feature.

The Game

Grand Dad Mania is based on a canceled SiIvaGunner game, which was basically a parody of Sonic Mania. It was originally featured as a high-quality rip in the "Trailer Theme (In-Game Mix) - Sonic Mania" video.
